COMPANY OVERVIEW  


From its founding in 1923, Ecolab’s story has been one of innovation, customer service, industry leadership and growth. Over the years, we have built upon our humble beginnings – one man transforming a simple idea into a simple product to cost-effectively clean hotel carpets – to become a global company with 40,000 employees serving more than one million customers across a range of industries in more than 160 countries.  














OUR OBJECTIVES  


At Ecolab, we make it a priority to know the unique cleaning and maintenance needs of our customers business and we pride ourselves on delivering excellence.  It’s why we offer customized products, programs and equipment backed by the industries largest and best-trained sales–and-service staff. It is also why we are the world leader in the development and marketing of innovative cleaning, sanitizing and maintenance products – and have been for almost 90 years.


We are dedicated to expanding our product offerings, safety and training features, thus significantly increasing our scope of specialist services to you - our valued business partner.

Ecolab’s objective is to provide Lugano Resort and Spa with Quality Products, Excellent Results and Consistent Service and Support at the very best possible cost hence our philosophy of “When you succeed we succeed”



ECOLAB INNOVATION LEADERSHIP 


Best of the Class Technology Matters! 


Ecolab is recognized as the best in industry by continuously launching best of the class innovation to help customers meet their emerging needs. Ecolab uses nothing but state of the art innovations focused at allowing you to run your business in the safest most efficient and effective manner, while helping your organization to become Cleaner, Safer, and Healthier in the process.  


Some of the innovation available are: 


  • SMARTPOWER provides more power, with less waste.  It is more sustainable than traditional product and reduces packaging waste by 99%.  Solid form reduces waste and storage spec, prevents spillage and misuse.   






  • OASIS PRO: Ecolab offers the new line of Biodegradable housekeeping product “OASIS PRO” which is the much more concentrated and also the chemical container is lockable to prevent waste and misuse.

















  • Wash n Walk: Deep packed-in grease and soil build-up over time create “grease banks" and Wash ’n Walk attacks this grease to eliminate slippery floors.  Wash ’n Walk reduces slips and falls, using cold water saves on energy and no rinse saves on water.







  • AQUANOMIC is Ecolab’s latest low temperature laundry chemistry that achieves premium results with lower energy, water consumption.  Laundry process is achieved while reducing temperatures a minimum of 25-30 degrees Celsius in wash wheel.
